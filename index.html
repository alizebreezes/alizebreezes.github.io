Happens For A Reason

I've often thought...
'Why can’t I stay in one place I like?, What am I looking for?'
It's been many different places where I have lived but nowhere actually could make me stay. I was wondering looking for something I didn't know what. The journey to find something looked endless wondering.

The pandemic, ironycally, gave me the life-changing turning point. I took an action to learn programming while I had a break and...

I’ve found myself who I only had dreamt about. I love what I am doing now and it’s awesome to feel certainty in the very present moment.

My past years of wondering, suddenly makes a lot of sense.
I have been not looking for a place to stay, I had been looking for something... I love to do no matter where I am.
